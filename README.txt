A Windows application with simple UI to trick your computer, 
including any one that you have access to via internet.
The app does not let a computer switch to sleep mode, 
by constantly jiggling your cursor in a random period of time.

After the app is launched, it is shown in Windows tray.
(If not, check hidden tray elements under the arrow button.)
Use right click to open contextual menu of the app. 

There are 3 options available: Start, Stop, and Exit.
With Start button, the cursor starts to move.
With Stop, the app pauses and the cursor no longer moves by its own.
With Exit, the app closes.
